About the Sun

The Sun is the center of the entire solar system, it's the main star that brings everything together. If the Sun suddenly disappeared, every planet would no longer orbit around the Sun and would instead move on its own out of the solar system and somewhere else, us humans will die if this were to ever occur in the future, though hopefully it never does.

It's a very bright star, if you were to travel close to it with a rocket ship, all you'd see is a bright giant circle. Furthermore, because the Sun is a star, it's also the one sole object that provides us humans on Earth with our light everyday. It is what makes mornings and daytime bright and noticeable whilst the Moon covers up the dark nights. It's also what makes the temperature during summer hot and warm and so forth.
