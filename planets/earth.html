About the Earth

Earth is the third planet in the solar system and from what we know is the only planet to exist that actually supports human life. Most of the planet is covered with water such as massive oceans, seas, gulfs and other things like lakes and rivers. Though not everything is covered with water, most of the other places is covered with land consisting of continents as well as islands with the final half being the polar regions that are just covered with straight ice.

Out of all the planets, our one is probably one of the most interesting ones. The fact that this is the only planet that supports any sign of life, shows to have any access to oxygen where we don't have to wear specially made suits in order to walk outside nor is there any other planet that shows any sign of life at all. The Earth is an amazing planet and hopefully it stays great for thousands of decades to come.
