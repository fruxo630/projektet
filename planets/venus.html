About Venus

Venus is the second planet in our solar system, it is named after a Roman goddess of love and beauty mainly because of the fact that it’s one of the brightest planets out there after the Moon. Looking at the planet it almost feels like you’re looking at a massive round ball filled with clouds. Venus is sometimes actually called Earth’s “little sister” because they share similar sizes and masses. Seeing as it’s a very bright planet, it has been made sacred to gods of many cultures as well as being a large inspiration for poets and writers.

Because Venus is relatively close to Earth, we’ve been using it a lot of times for space exploration but mainly general exploration of the different planets in our solar system. It was the first planet to go beyond Earth that was visited by a spacecraft and was also the first one ever to be successfully landed on.
