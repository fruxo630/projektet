About the Moon

The Moon could actually almost be considered as a "very small planet" though it's Earth's only "satellite" as it specifically travels around its own path in order to orbit around Earth instead of the Sun. The Moon can take on different shapes when it's in the sky and thus has a cycle of phases that have provided us humans with cultural references and influences throughout our history. These influences for example, can be found both in our language, calendars, art as well as mythology.

The other thing that makes the Moon so special to us humans is that its what replaces the Sun during nighttime. Our Moon provides us with light during the night when it's dark everywhere you'd look. Even if you can't see anything apart froms street lights when you're taking your car to get home after a long day, the Moon will always show a bright white light everytime you look up into the sky at night.
