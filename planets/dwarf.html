About the Dwarf planets

Dwarf planets are mostly considered very small "planets" that aren't big enough to even be called a planet. One prime example is Pluto, being a planet that used to be considered in the solar system but because of it being so small, was later changed into a dwarf planet and no longer deemed as an actual "planet". The other dwarf planets are Ceres, Eris, Makemake and Haumea though Pluto is still the biggest one out of the 5 dwarf planets.

Even though dwarf planets are very interesting because of them being relatively small yet being there, not all that much is known about these planets other than their small sizes.
